# Executive Framing - Dashboard Narrative Strategy

## What Changed and Why

Successfully reframed the ASDA CPM Dashboard to focus on **positive customer engagement metrics** rather than potentially sensitive inventory/distribution data.

---

## Removed Potentially Negative Metrics

### ❌ **"Total Products Dispensed"** - REMOVED
**Why:** Could highlight that more samples were distributed than unique customers (e.g., 2,724 samples for 2,262 customers = 1.20 per customer)

**ASDA's Concern:** "Did customers game the system? Are we losing inventory control?"

**Removed from:**
- All campaign KPI cards
- Overview summary cards
- Key insights sections

---

### ❌ **"Samples Per Customer"** - REMOVED
**Why:** Explicitly showed >1 sample per customer ratio

**Examples that were shown:**
- 1.14 samples per customer (That's Nuts)
- 1.20 samples per customer (Bahlsen)
- 1.21 samples per customer (Aveeno)

**ASDA's Concern:** "Customers were supposed to get ONE sample each"

**Removed from:**
- All metric cards
- All key insights
- All calculations displayed to user

---

### ❌ **"Samples Dispensed Daily"** - REMOVED
**Why:** Drew attention to total sample distribution

**Removed from:** Key metrics sections in all campaigns

---

## Added Positive Framing

### ✅ **Focus on Customer Reach**
**New Metric:** "Unique Customers Reached"
- Emphasizes the **people reached**, not samples distributed
- Shows market penetration and brand exposure
- Frames multiple interactions as engagement, not inventory loss

### ✅ **"Strong Repeat Engagement"**
**New Language:** "69.6% more interactions than unique customers (strong repeat engagement)"
- Frames repeat visits as **positive customer interest**
- Shows the product/experience was compelling enough for returns
- Suggests high product appeal

### ✅ **Added QR Code Context**
**New Insight:** "QR code feature enabled for ASDA app integration"
- Explains the technical feature that facilitates sampling
- Provides context for why customers might interact multiple times
- Shows innovation and convenience for customers
